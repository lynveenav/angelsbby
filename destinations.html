Popular Destinations in Siargao

Cloud 9 - Famous for its world-class surf breaks.

Sugba Lagoon - A stunning emerald-green lagoon perfect for kayaking and swimming.

Magpupungko Rock Pools - Natural tide pools with crystal-clear waters.

Guyam Island is a tiny island covered in palms.

Daku Island is the biggest island of the tour and a great place for a swim and lunch.

Naked Island is a small sandbar, which earned its name by literally just being sand.

Tayangban Cave Pool was one of the highlights of my many adventures throughout Siargao Island.

Tak Tak Falls is the only waterfall on Siargao Island, located in the province of Surigao del Norte in the Philippines.

Alegria Beach The first thing I noticed at Alegria Beach on Siargao was the white sand and crystal clear water.
